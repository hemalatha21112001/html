HISTORY

     A juice shop is a business that sells freshly squeezed and other types of fruit and vegetable juices, smoothies, and infused drinks. It can be a promising venture for entrepreneurs due to the increasing demand for healthy and convenient beverages, low initial investment, and potential for good profit margins. Success in this business relies on focusing on quality, innovating with recipes.

MISSION

     A juice business specializes in selling fruit and vegetable juices to their customers. They tend to focus on offering fresh, healthy beverages made from a variety of fruits and vegetables, and they may also offer smoothies, detox drinks, or wellness shots.
